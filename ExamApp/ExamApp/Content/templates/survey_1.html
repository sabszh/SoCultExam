Informed Consent Form

We are conducting an experiment investigating interaction between human and AI-chatbots. Your participation in this experiment is entirely voluntary, and you have the right to withdraw at any time without consequence. By agreeing to participate in this experiment, you are giving consent for us to collect the data you submit onto this webpage. To participate in this experiment, you must be 18 years or older. The collected data will be used exclusively for the purposes of this study and will be maintained confidential and anonymous. Your responses will not be linked to you in any way. If you have any questions about the experiment, you are welcome to send an email to Maria Mujemula Olsen (202106384@post.au.dk) or Sabrina Zaki Hansen (202105174@post.au.dk). However, please be aware that we may not be able to provide detailed information about the experiment before you have completed the experiment, as doing so could bias the results.
